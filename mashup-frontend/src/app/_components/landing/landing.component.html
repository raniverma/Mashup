About us

MASHUP is a Social Programming Engagement and Assessment Platform IT product to motivate programmers to try out new ideas and tech. Often programmers need the thrust and motivation to explore new technologies, exchange knowledge, try out new ideas, and keep raising their coding fluency. How can programming be made fun and engaging? How to encourage peer programming? How programmers of an organization rank up against one another? How programmers of an organization are rank up with other programmers across the world? How can programmers be recognized of their programming proficiencies in a standard way?

MASHUP is one-stop place for all the above !
